Data science lab research

The Data Science Lab at the Dong-A University aims at incorporating a knowledge graph into recommender systems. We pursue discovering potential paths between users and items discovered by exploring potential, dynamic interlinks within an evolving knowledge graph. We have applied various kinds of recommendation, analytics and reasoning techniques to real-time processing of data streams and (quasi-)static datasets in air quality watch, in smart energy monitoring, in location intelligence applications, in retail tech applications, in digital twin, in massive online open courses, and so on.
